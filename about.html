About Us

Aaron

I am Aaron. I started selling tomatoes in 1724. My tomatoes taste the best. Unfortunately back in the day I could only grow one tomato a year. Then I met Yohannes in 1992. He showed me the way. With his innovative ideas we are now able to grow and sell anywhere between 33-84 tomatoes in a year. My mission is to put one tomato on every dinner table on the planet.

I was Yohannes. I met Aaron in 1992 and saw his impressive farm. I thought how could I improve this thens when I got the idea to plant more tomatoes in his farm. At first I thought it would fail but luck was on my side as production soared to over 30 tomatoes. One year we even got 84 tomatoes. Now I we're working on glow in the dark tomatoes so you can eat them even at night.
